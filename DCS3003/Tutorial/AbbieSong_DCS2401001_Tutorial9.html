    • Lanling King swings his fist blade at enemies around him, causing physical damage. At the same time, he summons a shadow clone to randomly attack a nearby enemy, which will cause the same amount of damage.

  3. Secret Technique: Shadow Eclipse
      4. Lanling King casts a shadow dagger in the specified direction, dealing physical damage to enemies on the path and reducing their movement speed by 90% for 2 seconds. During this period, if they are attacked by Lanling King again, they will be stunned for 1 second and their health will be restored. When the mark disappears, the target will also receive physical damage equal to 24% of the lost health. When the dagger hits an enemy hero, it will stop moving and be destroyed.

    • Charge in the specified direction and cause physical damage to enemies on the path

  5. Secret Technique: Hidden
    • Lanling King starts to prepare and enters stealth state after 1.5 seconds, which lasts for 30 seconds. After invisibility, he gains a 20% movement speed bonus which lasts for 5 seconds. After the invisibility is released, Lanling King's attack speed will increase by 50% which lasts for 5 seconds. If he stays near the enemy for more than 3 seconds while in stealth, the invisibility will be automatically released.

The story of LanLing

Drought and war have turned the once prosperous country into a desert. Under the ruins lies a forgotten ancient country.

Few people can imagine how glorious this place was in the past, but he will never forget it. Lanling, once a noble prince, is now a death god lurking in the darkness.

When he was still a child, he saw with his own eyes the imperial cavalry trample the city walls of the capital, and his parents jumped from the tower, sowing the seeds of hatred.

The old man in the mountain took him away and trained him to be a killer. He hid in the darkness.Many times he was hired by powerful people to assassinate peace envoys, provoke conflicts, and cleverly stir up fear.

However, Lanling, who abandoned his noble status, never gave up the idea of ​​revenge. He had to use this method to express his grief for the past, otherwise the darkness that expanded in his heart would eventually devour him.

What stood in his way was an iron wall - the Great Wall. As long as the Great Wall stood, the empire would not fall easily. The Great Wall was indestructible, and only its guardians could break it.

Countless times, Lanling climbed up the high wall to spy on the secrets of the fortress.

He knew the names, origins, factions and conflicts of the guards. Lanling had been patiently looking for their fatal weaknesses and adding fuel to the fire when necessary.

The commander fell, the misunderstood traitor fled, and the guards were leaderless. The chaos was short-lived, but a moment was enough. At least, it was so for the barbarians who got the news and were waiting for the opportunity. Their attack turned the Great Wall into a hell of blood and fire.

One person against an entire country, how absurd and yet how simple.

He repeated this trick, spreading death. Fear grew, and the garrison at the Wall was manipulated. Until one night, the edge of another sword pierced through the darkness.

"It's you, Ghost!"

LanLing smiled.

Well, winning all the time is pretty boring.

"The land where the blade cuts is the territory."
