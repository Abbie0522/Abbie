Travel Tips
Pack Smart:
Use a packing list and pack light especially if you'll be moving around frequently.

Keep Essentials in Your Carry-On:
Always have essentials like medications, chargers, a change of clothes and toiletries in your carry-on.

Use Digital Copies of Important Documents:
Take photos or scan your passport, tickets, IDs and any other important documents then store them securely in cloud storage and keep physical copies separately from the originals.

Notify Your Bank:
Inform your bank of your travel dates and destinations to prevent any issues with your credit or debit card being frozen due to "suspicious" activity abroad.

Stay Hydrated and Prioritize Sleep:
Traveling can disrupt your usual routine, so stay hydrated and get plenty of rest to keep your energy up and your immune system strong.

Be Cautious with WIFI:
Avoid using public WIFI for sensitive transactions like banking.

Embrace Flexibility:
Travel can be unpredictable, so stay open-minded and adaptable.
